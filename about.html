🤝

Our Mission

Every year, millions of items get lost across our communities — wallets, keys, pets, phones, and much more. Most of these items could be reunited with their owners if there was a reliable, accessible platform to connect finders with owners.

The Community Lost & Found Portal was created to fill that gap. We're a free, open platform where anyone can post a lost or found item, search for matches, and connect directly with their neighbours to make things right.

Process

How It Works

From posting to recovery in just a few simple steps.

1

Create an Account

Sign up with your email or Google account. Verified accounts build trust in our community.

2

Post Your Item

Upload a photo, add a description, location and date. Your post goes live instantly.

3

Search & Match

Use our search and filter tools to find matching items posted by others in your area.

4

Connect Directly

Contact the poster directly via their email to arrange a safe meeting and return.

Community Standards

Community Guidelines

To keep our platform safe and effective, please follow these guidelines.

✅

Be Honest & Accurate

Post accurate descriptions and honest photos. Misleading posts harm the community.

🔒

Protect Privacy

Do not share sensitive personal information publicly. Use our contact system for private communication.

🤝

Verify Before Claiming

Ask specific questions to verify ownership before handing over any item to someone claiming it.

⚡

Respond Promptly

Check your notifications and respond to inquiries quickly. Time matters when reuniting lost items.

🚫

No Fraudulent Posts

Fake posts, scams, or attempts to profit from found items are strictly prohibited and will result in removal.

🏛️

Report Suspicious Activity

If you see suspicious posts or behaviour, report it immediately. We rely on the community to stay safe.

📸

Use Clear Photos

Upload clear, well-lit photos of items to help people identify whether it matches what they're looking for.

🗑️

Remove Resolved Posts

Once your item is found or returned, please mark it as resolved or remove the post to keep listings current.
